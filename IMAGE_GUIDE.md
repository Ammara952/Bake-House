## üéØ Step-by-Step Download Guide

### From Unsplash:
1. Go to https://unsplash.com/
2. Search for the term (e.g., "chocolate cake")
3. Click on the image you like
4. Click "Download free" button
5. Rename the file to match the required name
6. Move to the correct folder

### From Pexels:
1. Go to https://www.pexels.com/
2. Search for the term
3. Click on the image
4. Click "Download" button
5. Rename and move to correct folder

### From Pixabay:
1. Go to https://pixabay.com/
2. Search for the term
3. Click on the image
4. Click "Free Download"
5. Select size and download
6. Rename and move to correct folder
